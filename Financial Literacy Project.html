What are some risks of using a credit card?

Some risks in using a credit card are...

  • A credit card is easy to use so you might use it a lot.
  • When using a credit card, you always have to pay back the money you use.
  • Credit cards have interest and the interest might be too much for you to pay.

What happens when you pay only the minimum payment?

When you only pay the minimum amount, your credit score starts to drop which means that the next time you take out a loan, the interest is going to be really high. This is what makes credit cards difficult to pay off.

If you were to give advice to a friend who was thinking about getting a credit card, what advice would you give him/her?

Advice that I would give him/her is always wait, it might be better to start having a debit card which you do not have to pay or pay interest and it is less risky, or maybe open a savings account which is better and safer.
